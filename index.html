The Journey

Science, Education, and Faith combined.

My professional journey is rooted in a passion for both science and theology. As an A-Grade Pharmacist, I have a deep respect for medical sciences and healthcare disciplines.

I channel this knowledge into education, exclusively tutoring young women in SSC and HSC Biology and Chemistry. My goal is to make complex scientific concepts accessible and inspire the next generation of female scientists and doctors.

Beyond the sciences, I am blessed to connect with families internationally. Through online platforms, I teach the Quran to children in Finland, Denmark, and Germany, ensuring that distance is not a barrier to proper Islamic education.
